Statistics on Light Pollution

Light pollution is on the rise, and the following statistics illustrate its growing impact:

  • 80% of the world's population: Lives under light-polluted skies, with the Milky Way invisible to one-third of humanity.
  • Over 60%: Of North Americans can no longer see the Milky Way from their homes.
  • 99%: Of people in the U.S. and Europe live under light-polluted skies.
  • 2% per year: The rate at which artificial light is increasing globally.

Light Pollution Map

The Light Pollution Map provides a detailed visualization of light pollution levels worldwide. It uses satellite data to show the extent of artificial light at night, highlighting areas with the most severe light pollution. This map is a valuable tool for understanding the global impact of light pollution and identifying areas where action is needed.
